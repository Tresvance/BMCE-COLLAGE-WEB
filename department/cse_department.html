About Our Department

Founded in 1985, the Department of Computer Science at University College has established itself as a center of excellence in computer science education and research. Our department is committed to advancing the frontiers of computing knowledge through innovative research and providing high-quality education that prepares students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving field of technology.

We offer undergraduate and graduate programs that blend theoretical foundations with practical skills, ensuring our students are well-equipped to address complex challenges in the digital world. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect emerging technologies and industry trends, providing students with relevant and cutting-edge knowledge.

Our faculty members are distinguished researchers and educators who bring extensive expertise in various fields of computer science, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, data science, software engineering, computer networks, and human-computer interaction. They are dedicated to mentoring students and fostering a collaborative learning environment.

The department maintains state-of-the-art laboratories and computing facilities that support teaching, research, and innovation. We have strong connections with industry partners, creating opportunities for internships, collaborative research, and employment for our graduates.
